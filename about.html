World of Hospitality / About

The people behind the rooms you remember.

A Doha-based F&B management group operating and developing premium international brands across Qatar.

Our story, briefly.

World of Hospitality was built by operators who'd spent a long time on the floor before they ever sat in a boardroom. We knew the difference between a restaurant that opens well and one that lasts — and we knew that difference had less to do with the menu than with the hundred small decisions made between 4pm and 4am.

So we built a company designed for those decisions. We bring established international F&B brands to Qatar, adapt them carefully to the local market, and operate them with senior people in the room every single day. Our partners — including MK Group and Günaydın Et — give us access to concepts that have already proven themselves at home. Our team gives them somewhere to land that doesn't dilute what made them work.

We're a Qatari company by location and license. We're an international company by network, training, and standards. We think those are the only two things you have to be.

"A restaurant is a thousand small promises made every night.
Our job is to keep all of them."
— Founding principle

How we actually work.

Three commitments that show up in how the day runs — not just how it reads on a brand deck.

01 / Brand

Origin first.

The original recipe, the original room, the original feel. We adapt for the market; we don't water down for it. Local insight is a refinement, not a rewrite.

02 / Operations

Senior people, every shift.

Our floor managers, executive chefs, and finance leads aren't hired into a system — they shape it. The people accountable for the result are the people in the building.

03 / Growth

Built to scale, sized to care.

Shared back-office and supply give us the cost structure of a group; deliberate operating teams give us the attention of an independent.

Leadership

Operators, not consultants.

Our leadership team has opened, run, and turned around restaurants across the Gulf, the Levant, and Europe. We're naming names as we publish full bios — until then, ask us about anyone on the team and we'll tell you exactly what they've built.
