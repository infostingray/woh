World of Hospitality / About

The people behind the rooms you remember.

A Doha-based F&B management group operating and developing premium international brands across Qatar.

Our story, briefly.

World of Hospitality is run by operators. We bring established international F&B brands to Qatar, adapt them carefully to the market, and run them with senior people in the room every day.

Our partners — including MK Group and Günaydın Et — give us access to concepts already proven in their home markets. Our team gives them somewhere to land that doesn't dilute what made them work.

Our job is to keep all of them."
