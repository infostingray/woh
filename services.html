World of Hospitality / Services

End to end. Same team. No handoffs.

From the first site visit to the thousandth Tuesday lunch service — what we do, in the order we do it.

01

Concept & brand strategy

We assess concepts for fit with the Qatari market, identify what to keep untouched and what to refine, and build the local positioning that gets the brand seen properly from day one.

02

Site selection & lease negotiation

Trade-area analysis, footfall studies, landlord relationships across Doha's malls, hotels, and standalone districts. We bring you the shortlist that's actually right — not the one that's available.

03

Design & fit-out management

We work with brand-approved designers or your own team. Local contractor selection, permits, MEP, kitchen specification, FF&E procurement, and a project timeline we actually hold to.

04

Licensing & regulatory

Commercial registration, municipality approvals, food safety, labour permits, alcohol licensing where applicable. The paperwork that quietly decides whether you open on time.

05

Recruitment & training

Senior hires first — executive chef, GM, operations lead — then the rest of the team built around them. Pre-opening training runs to the brand's own standards, taught by people who've taught it before.

06

Pre-opening & launch

Soft-launch programming, PR strategy, influencer and media relations, opening-week service ops. The first six weeks set the next six years; we treat them that way.

07

Daily operations

Service standards, kitchen discipline, supplier management, inventory, scheduling, guest experience. Weekly operating reviews. Monthly performance against budget. Quarterly brand health.

08

Finance & reporting

Centralised finance, payroll, P&L, KPI dashboards, and the kind of reporting brand owners back home can actually use. No mystery in the numbers.

09

Marketing & growth

In-market campaigns, loyalty, partnerships, and the slow work of building a guest base that returns. When the time comes — and only then — we plan the next location.

Engagement models

Three ways to work together.

Franchise & operate. We hold the local license and run the venue end-to-end on your brand's standards.

Management contract. You own the venue — we run it on your behalf, with full operational accountability.

Joint venture. Aligned capital, aligned outcomes, aligned timelines. The strongest model for long-horizon brands.
